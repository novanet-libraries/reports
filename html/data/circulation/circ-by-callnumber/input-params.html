  • Field Info
  • Sublibrary - Pick one or more Novanet libraries.
  • Period - Pick the start and end dates during which circulation will be counted.
  • Subdivide Selected Period - If 'no', you will get a total count for the entire period. If 'yes', then you will get counts for individual months or years or days within the period.
  • Call Number Range - Enter one or more LC call number subject areas, e.g. H1-99 or D-F or HG. You will get circ counts for each subject area you enter, as well as a count of items outside of your LC ranges and a count of items that have non-LC callnumbers. If your ranges overlap with each other, some circulation events will be counted multiple times (e.g. "H" is interepreted as all social sciences, H-HX, so "H" on one line and "HF" on the next counts HF twice).
